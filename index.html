About

My name is Julia Ballas. I am a writer and graphic designer. In 2018 I became a non-traditional student, returning to school to study visual and media design. My first degree was in English with a focus on creative writing. Now, I explore the visual side of creativity, using shapes and color for storytelling.

I am inspired by my surroundings, by the breathtaking beauty of the Northwestern and Montana landscapes, but I also have a passion for traveling the world. I surround myself with bright bold colors from jade green to indigo blue.
