About

My name is Julia Ballas. I am a writer and graphic designer inspired by my surroundings. In 2018 I became a non-traditional student, returning to school to study visual and media design. My first degree is in English with a focus on creative writing. Now, I explore the visual side of creativity, using shapes and color to enhance my storytelling.

Living in the Northwestern USA and Montana has stolen my imagination, the forests, mountains and bright blue skies. I love to surround myself with bright bold colors from spring green to sky blue.
