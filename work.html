Let's make Ice Cream

Ice + Salt + Armpower + homemade custard = Yum!

Custard Ice Cream

  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 8 egg yolks, slightly beaten
  • 4 cups milk, heat if you’re infusing or melting chocolate
  • 2 pints cream
  • 1 TBSP vanilla
  1. 1. Mix sugar, salt and egg yolks and milk.
  2. 2. Cook over medium to med low heat until the mixture coats a spoon.
  3. 3. Cool, strain and add cream and other flavorings.
  4. 4. Freeze

(Makes 6 pints in hand crank mixer.)

Sources: Franny Farmer 1965. Serious Eats: www.seriouseats.com/how-many-eggs-should-i-use-to-make-ice-cream and www.seriouseats.com/ice-cream-myths
